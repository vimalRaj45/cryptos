CryptoSign

System Architecture

Follow the step-by-step process to understand how Digital Signatures ensure authenticity and integrity.

1
Identity Registration

Register a unique username which serves as your identity within the ecosystem.

2
RSA Key Generation

Generates a 2048-bit Private Key (kept secret) and a Public Key (shared with everyone).

3
Message Signing

Hashes the message using SHA-256 and encrypts it with your Private Key using RSA-PSS padding.

4
Signature Verification

Anyone with your Public Key can verify that the message was indeed signed by you and remains untampered.
